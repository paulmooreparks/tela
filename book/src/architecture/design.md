# Why a connectivity fabric

This chapter is the design rationale for Tela's core architecture: why the project ships as three small daemons rather than one, why the hub is a blind relay, why WireGuard runs in userspace through gVisor, and what alternatives were considered and rejected. The body of the book teaches what Tela is and how to use it; this chapter answers why it is built this way.
