# Self-update and release channels

Every Tela binary can update itself in place. This chapter explains the three release channels (dev, beta, stable), how to inspect and switch between them, and how to keep hubs and agents current without redeploying by hand. Read it once when you set up your first hub; refer back when you are ready to move from dev to a more conservative channel.
